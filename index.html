The Atelier

Alchemists spent lifetimes trying to turn base material into gold. We settled for something more modest: turning a sketch on a napkin into a sticker you'll actually keep on your laptop.

Every design here is made by Leighric or a friend who agreed to let their weird little drawing loose into the world. We print in small runs, on things we'd want ourselves — starting with stickers and deskmats, with more items joining the cabinet as we go.
